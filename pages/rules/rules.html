Rules
  1. You can't start the game if you haven't written your name.
  2. If the answer is correct, 1 point is added; otherwise, 1 point is deducted.
  3. There are two modes to play:
    1. Practice Mode

      In this mode, you can endlessly solve examples.

      If you want to end the game, press the button to stop the game

    2. Time Attack Mode

      You have 1 minute 30 seconds to solve the examples

      you can see the timer, how much time do you have left

  4. Examples can be with 4 operations (addition, subtraction, division, multiplication) Press enter to submit your answer.
